Priority Limousine Collection ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how your personal information is collected, used, and protected in compliance with the Singapore Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA).

1. Information We Collect

When you request a quote or make a booking, we collect necessary information including your name, contact number, email address, flight details, and pickup/drop-off locations to facilitate your journey.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used strictly for operational purposes: to dispatch chauffeurs, monitor flight arrivals, process billing, and communicate with you regarding your reservation.

3. Data Protection & Confidentiality

We maintain strict discretion for all VIP and corporate clients. Your itinerary, destination, and personal details are never shared with third parties, except where necessary for payment processing or required by Singapore law.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding how we handle your personal data, please contact our administrative team at reservations@prioritylimousine.sg.
