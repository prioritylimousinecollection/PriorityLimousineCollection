1. Bookings & Confirmations

All reservations are subject to vehicle availability. A booking is only considered confirmed once you receive an official confirmation via email or WhatsApp from our dispatch team.

2. Waiting Time Policy

For Changi Airport arrivals, we provide a complimentary 60-minute waiting time starting from the actual flight landing time. For point-to-point transfers and corporate roadshows, a complimentary 15-minute grace period is provided. Waiting time exceeding these allowances will incur additional hourly or block charges.

3. Vehicle Substitution

While we strive to provide the exact model requested (e.g., Toyota Alphard 40 Series, Mercedes S-Class), we reserve the right to upgrade or substitute the vehicle with one of equal or higher premium standing in the rare event of mechanical failure or unforeseen circumstances.

4. Cancellations

Cancellations made within 24 hours of the scheduled pickup time may be subject to a cancellation fee. No-shows will be charged at the full rate of the booked journey.
